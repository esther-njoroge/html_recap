Company Presentation

Elimutab is an educational tablet designed specifically for students.
It comes with educational apps, books and resources to help students learn .
The company was passionate about improving the quality education in Kenya and beyond.
The tablets have parental control features to protect children from inappropriate content.

Advantages of the elimutab

  1. Its easy to use.
  2. Protects children from inappropriate content.
  3. It has time management features that enable parents control the amount of time kids spend on the devices.
  4. It has a battery life of upto 10hours on a single charge.
